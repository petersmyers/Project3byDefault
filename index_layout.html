Loan Default Predictor

Life is full of risks and chances... Lets not make paying off loans one of them.

About

Congratulations on receiving a loan

Now you have the pleasure of paying if off :(

While banks are legally required to be unbiased in their lending practices, predatory lending practices are not extinct. Unfortunately, individuals of lower socio-economic standing are more likely to get behind in their loan repayments.

We have developed a neural network model using TensorFlow in Python that predicts the outcome of a loan (i.e. will you pay it off or default?). This model is built from data provided by LendingClub.com using over 250,000 data points.
